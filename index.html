Particle/Arduino RC car project

This is my second real electronics project. I've always thought it would be fun to wire up a computer to an RC can and program it to drive around. Attempt number one was many years ago. It involved sticking wires directly into the parallel port of a 386 PC and clipping the other ends onto the RC controller circuit board. It was programmed in QBasic. It worked! ...until it circuit board ran out of magic smoke:(

Attempt number two will be a little more methodical and documented here!

1: Connect the RC car to the Arduino

2: Get the Particle board to talk to the Arduino

3: Program the Arduino to expose an API for driving the car via serial

4: Program the Particle board to expose a web interface for controlling the RC car

5: Program the Particle board to relay commands to the Arduino

6: Teach the Arduino to detect its environment...
